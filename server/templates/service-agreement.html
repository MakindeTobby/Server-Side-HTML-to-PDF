1. Purpose and Scope
1.1 This Service Agreement sets out the terms and conditions under which the Service Provider will deliver support services to the Participant in accordance with their NDIS plan and goals.
1.2 The Service Provider is committed to delivering high-quality, person-centered services that respect the Participant's choices, dignity, and rights.
2. Services to be Provided
2.1 The Service Provider agrees to provide the following services:
Service Type Description Frequency Support Item Number
Personal Care Assistance with daily personal activities 5 days/week 01_011_0107_1_1
Community Access Support to participate in community activities 2 days/week 04_104_0125_6_1
Household Tasks Assistance with domestic duties 3 days/week 01_012_0120_1_1
2.2 Services will be delivered at locations agreed upon between the Participant and Service Provider, including:
Participant's residence
Community venues
Other locations as mutually agreed
3. Service Delivery Standards
3.1 All services will be delivered in accordance with the NDIS Practice Standards and the NDIS Code of Conduct.
3.2 The Service Provider will ensure all support workers are appropriately qualified, trained, and hold current NDIS Worker Screening Clearances.
3.3 Services will be person-centered and delivered with respect for the Participant's dignity, privacy, and independence.
4. Fees and Payment
4.1 All services will be charged in accordance with the current NDIS Price Guide and will be claimed directly from the Participant's NDIS plan.
4.2 The Participant will be provided with invoices detailing services delivered, including dates, times, and support item numbers.
4.3 Payment terms are 14 days from the date of invoice, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
5. Participant Rights and Responsibilities
5.1 The Participant has the right to:
Be treated with dignity and respect
Make decisions about their own supports
Provide feedback and make complaints
Access their personal information
Terminate this agreement with reasonable notice
5.2 The Participant agrees to:
Provide accurate information about their needs and circumstances
Provide reasonable notice of cancellations
Treat support workers with respect
Inform the Service Provider of any changes to their NDIS plan
6. Complaints and Feedback
6.1 The Participant may provide feedback or make a complaint at any time by contacting the Service Provider's Complaints Officer.
6.2 All complaints will be handled in accordance with the Service Provider's Complaints Management Policy and the NDIS Practice Standards.
7. Termination
7.1 Either party may terminate this agreement by providing 14 days written notice.
7.2 The Service Provider may terminate this agreement immediately if the Participant's behavior poses a risk to the safety of support workers or others.
8. Privacy and Confidentiality
8.1 The Service Provider will handle all personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles.
8.2 Personal information will only be shared with third parties with the Participant's consent or as required by law.
